Navigate to "Register for exams" from the sidebar, select your department, choose courses, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation code to complete the registration.

All exam registrations are stored on a blockchain to ensure transparency, integrity, and immutable record-keeping. Each registration receives a unique hash that can be verified.

We implement role-based access control, data classification (public, private, confidential, restricted), and GDPR compliance to protect your information. All passwords are securely hashed.
